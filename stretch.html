STRETCH MEDINA!

FACIAL STRETCH THERAPY (Assisted Stretch) is a type of bodywork that involves slow, controlled movements to stretch and lengthen the fascia, a network of connective tissue that surrounds and supports the muscles, bones, and organs in the body.

This technique can have a number of benefits for both physical and mental health. One of the main benefits of fascial stretch is improved flexibility and range of motion.

By releasing tension and adhesions in the fascia, the muscles and joints are able to move more freely and easily. This can help to reduce pain and stiffness, and can be especially beneficial for athletes, dancers, and other people who engage in physical activity. Fascial stretch can also help to improve posture and alignment.

By releasing tension in the fascia, the body is able to move into a more balanced and natural position, which can help to reduce strain and discomfort in the neck, shoulders, back, and hips. Another benefit of fascial stretch is improved circulation and lymphatic flow. As the fascia is stretched and manipulated, blood and lymphatic fluids are able to flow more freely throughout the body, which can help to nourish tissues, remove waste products, and support the immune system.

Overall, fascial stretch is a safe and effective way to improve flexibility, posture, and circulation, and can provide a range of physical and mental health benefits.

If you're interested in trying fascial stretch for yourself, feel free to contact me. I can guide you through the process and help you achieve the best possible results.
