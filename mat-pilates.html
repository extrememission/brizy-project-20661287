MAT PILATES

Introducing our new Mat Pilates class at Reform & Restore Pilates in Medina, Ohio

Dive into a refreshing workout that combines the core-strengthening benefits of Pilates with the versatility of mat exercises. Designed to complement our awesome Reformer sessions, this new class focuses on body control, flexibility, and endurance using only a mat, your own body weight, and props (which include light dumbbells, the Pilates ring, a small ball, and a foam roller). You will love this!


Whether you are a seasoned Pilates enthusiast or new to Pilates, our experienced instructors will guide you through an innovative flow to enhance your core strength, balance, and overall well-being.

Join us and experience a dynamic and invigorating workout that will leave you feeling revitalized and empowered!

Classes are 50 minutes in duration

Mats and props are provided

Please bring your own water bottle
